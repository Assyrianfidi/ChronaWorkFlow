# AccuBooks Go-To-Market Execution Plan - $0 → $50K ARR

## 1️⃣ IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILES (ICPs)

### ICP #1: Overwhelmed Solopreneur
**Profile**: Freelancers, consultants, solo practitioners
**Revenue**: $50K - $300K annually
**Current Tool**: QuickBooks Simple Start or Self-Employed
**Pain Points**:
- Spending 5-10 hours monthly on manual bookkeeping
- Missing tax deductions due to poor categorization
- Cash flow surprises because no forecasting
- QuickBooks keeps raising prices without adding value

**Why They Abandon QuickBooks**:
- Price increases from $15 → $25 → $30 for same features
- No automation for repetitive tasks
- Mobile app is clunky and slow
- Customer support is non-existent

**Buying Triggers**:
- QuickBooks price increase notification
- Tax season stress and missed deductions
- Hiring first contractor/employee
- Reaching $250K+ revenue

**Target Message**: "Stop wasting 10 hours monthly on bookkeeping. Our AI does it for you."

---

### ICP #2: Growing Small Business Owner
**Profile**: 2-10 employee businesses
**Revenue**: $300K - $2M annually
**Current Tool**: QuickBooks Plus or Essentials
**Industries**: Professional services, e-commerce, local services
**Pain Points**:
- Need 5+ user seats but QuickBooks Plus only includes 5
- Manual categorization of 100+ transactions monthly
- No real-time cash flow visibility
- Payroll is separate system causing sync issues

**Why They Abandon QuickBooks**:
- Forced to upgrade to Advanced ($200/mo) for 6th user
- Bank feeds constantly break and require manual fixing
- No automation for repetitive invoicing/billing
- Reporting is basic and requires Excel workarounds

**Buying Triggers**:
- Hiring 6th employee (user seat limit hit)
- Monthly transaction volume exceeds 200
- Need multi-entity or project tracking
- QuickBooks quotes $200+/month for Advanced

**Target Message**: "Get twice the users and AI automation for $9 more than QuickBooks Plus."

---

### ICP #3: QuickBooks Power User Maxed Out
**Profile**: 10-25 employee businesses
**Revenue**: $2M - $5M annually
**Current Tool**: QuickBooks Advanced or considering Enterprise
**Industries**: Manufacturing, professional services, tech companies
**Pain Points**:
- QuickBooks Advanced at $200/month still lacks AI insights
- Complex reporting requires expensive add-ons
- No predictive analytics or forecasting
- Integration limitations forcing custom development

**Why They Abandon QuickBooks**:
- QuickBooks Enterprise starts at $850/month (4x price jump)
- No native AI or automation capabilities
- Poor mobile experience for field teams
- Limited API causing integration headaches

**Buying Triggers**:
- QuickBooks Enterprise quote shock ($850+/month)
- Need advanced reporting and analytics
- Expanding to multiple entities
- Competitors using modern tools gaining efficiency

**Target Message**: "Enterprise features at 1/3 the price with AI QuickBooks can't match."

## 3️⃣ FUNNEL DESIGN

### Landing Page → Trial → Activation → Paid

**Landing Page (Top of Funnel)**
- Headline: "Switch from QuickBooks in 15 Minutes. Save 10+ Hours Monthly."
- Key benefits: AI automation, cost savings, migration guarantee
- Social proof: 1,200+ businesses switched
- CTA: "Start Free 14-Day Trial" (primary) / "See Migration Demo" (secondary)

**Trial Sign-up (Middle of Funnel)**
- Minimal form: Email + password only
- Immediate value proposition: "Import your QuickBooks data now"
- Progress indicator: 3-step onboarding
- Account creation → Company setup → Data import

**Activation Milestones**:
- **Day 1**: Complete data import, view first AI-categorized transactions
- **Day 3**: Create first invoice or run first report
- **Day 7**: Use AI CFO Copilot for first financial query
- **Day 14**: Experience full automation benefits

**Paid Conversion (Bottom of Funnel)**
- Trigger: Day 12 email with migration completion summary
- Offer: "Your trial ends in 48 hours. Choose your plan:"
- Urgency: Limited-time launch discount
- Friction removal: One-click plan selection

**Drop-off Points & Solutions**:
- **Data Import Failure**: Live chat support, guaranteed fix
- **Day 3 No Activity**: Automated email with "Quick win" tutorial
- **Day 7 No AI Usage**: Personal outreach from success team
- **Trial End No Conversion**: Extended trial + personalized demo

## 1. Product Packaging Strategy

### 1.1 Tier Architecture

**FREE Tier ($0)**
- **Target**: Individuals, freelancers, micro-businesses
- **Features**: Basic accounting, invoicing, expense tracking
- **Limits**: 1 user, 1 company, 50 transactions/month, 1GB storage
- **Growth Path**: Usage-based triggers for Starter upgrade
- **Conversion Rate Target**: 15%

**STARTER Tier ($29/month, $290/year)**
- **Target**: Small businesses (1-50 employees)
- **Features**: Multi-user (3 users), basic compliance, API access, email support
- **Limits**: 3 users, 2 companies, 500 transactions/month, 10GB storage
- **Growth Path**: Team size and usage triggers for Pro upgrade
- **Conversion Rate Target**: 25%

**PRO Tier ($99/month, $990/year)**
- **Target**: Medium businesses (51-500 employees)
- **Features**: Advanced compliance, custom integrations, priority support, audit logs
- **Limits**: 10 users, 5 companies, 5K transactions/month, 100GB storage
- **Growth Path**: Enterprise compliance and scale triggers
- **Conversion Rate Target**: 20%

**ENTERPRISE Tier ($499/month, $4,990/year + $5K setup)**
- **Target**: Enterprise (500+ employees)
- **Features**: Unlimited everything, enterprise governance, custom compliance
- **Limits**: None - unlimited scale
- **Growth Path**: Custom solutions and strategic partnerships
- **Conversion Rate Target**: 35%

## üîí TRUST FRAMING: HOW ACCUBOOKS AVOIDS BULLSHIT FORECASTS

### The Problem with Most Forecasting Tools

**Black box syndrome**: You enter numbers, get a forecast, have no idea how it was calculated.

**Overconfidence**: Tools show "Runway: 18 months" with no uncertainty, no caveats.

**Garbage in, garbage out**: Bad assumptions produce confident-looking but useless forecasts.

---

### How AccuBooks Is Different

**1. Show Your Work**

Every forecast has a "Calculation Explainer" button. Click it, see the formula:

```
Runway = Cash Reserves / Monthly Burn Rate
Monthly Burn Rate = Expenses - Revenue
```

No magic. No AI guessing. Just math you can verify.

---

**2. Honest About Uncertainty**

Every forecast shows a confidence score:

- **85% confidence**: Strong data, realistic assumptions
- **68% confidence**: Moderate data, check your assumptions
- **52% confidence**: Weak data or optimistic assumptions‚Äîbe careful

We tell you when to trust the numbers and when to dig deeper.

---

**3. Assumptions You Control**

You set the assumptions (revenue growth, churn rate, expense increases). We calculate the outcomes.

If your assumptions are wrong, the forecast is wrong. But at least you know what you assumed.

**Example**:
- You assume 5% monthly revenue growth
- AccuBooks shows confidence: 52% (optimistic)
- You adjust to 2% growth
- Confidence increases to 73% (realistic)

You're in control. We're just doing the math.

---

**4. Built for Skeptics**

We assume you don't trust us. That's why:
- ‚úÖ All calculations are transparent
- ‚úÖ All assumptions are editable
- ‚úÖ All data is exportable (CSV, no lock-in)
- ‚úÖ No proprietary formulas or secret sauce

If you want to verify in Excel, go ahead. The math will match.
